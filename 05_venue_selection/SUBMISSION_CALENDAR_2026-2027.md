## ARR (ACL Rolling Review)

ARR is the rolling review system for ACL-family conferences (ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, EACL).
Submit once; commit to a specific conference at the commitment deadline.

| Cycle | Submission deadline | Reviews due | Commit by | Eligible for |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **June 2026** | ~June 15, 2026 | ~Aug 15 | Sep 1 | EMNLP 2026 Findings/Main |
| **August 2026** | ~Aug 15, 2026 | ~Oct 15 | Nov 1 | EMNLP 2026 Findings/Main, NAACL 2027 |
| **October 2026** | ~Oct 15, 2026 | ~Dec 15 | Jan 2027 | NAACL 2027, ACL 2027 |
| **December 2026** | ~Dec 15, 2026 | ~Feb 2027 | Mar 2027 | NAACL 2027, ACL 2027 |
| **February 2027** | ~Feb 15, 2027 | ~Apr 2027 | May 2027 | ACL 2027, EMNLP 2027 |
| **April 2027** | ~Apr 15, 2027 | ~Jun 2027 | Jul 2027 | EMNLP 2027 |

**T02 target**: June 2026 ARR cycle → EMNLP 2026 Findings (December 2026).
If T02 misses June: aim for August cycle. If August misses: pivot to TMLR.

**Verify**: https://aclrollingreview.org

## TMLR (Transactions on Machine Learning Research)

**Rolling**, no deadline. Submit when ready.

| Item | Typical timeline |
|---|---|
| Submission | Any time |
| Action editor assignment | 1–2 weeks |
| Reviews | 6–10 weeks |
| Decision | 8–14 weeks total |
| Camera-ready | 1–4 weeks |
| Publication | 1–2 weeks after camera-ready |

**APC**: $0
**Indexing**: DBLP, Google Scholar, OpenReview
**Preprints**: encouraged
**Best for**: T02 fallback, T07 primary, T01 fallback, B05 fallback

**Verify**: https://www.jmlr.org/tmlr/

## Workflow

For each paper:
1. Check this calendar in week 1 of execution.
2. Pick a primary deadline target.
3. Set milestones backwards from deadline.
4. At week 12 (1 month before deadline): commit to submission target or shift.
5. After submission: log in `DECISIONS.md`.

## Anti-recommendations (do not submit here)

| Venue | Reason |
|---|---|
| IEEE Access | High APC ($1,995); low prestige for NLP/eval topics |
| PLOS ONE | $2,000+ APC; not respected for ML |
| Frontiers in AI | $2,500 APC; spam-publish reputation in ML |
| MDPI journals | Predatory perception; avoid even if technically peer-reviewed |
| Any "International Journal of …" with APC | Default-suspicious |
| Conferences accepting "as long as you pay registration" | Avoid |

When in doubt, run venue through Think.Check.Submit (https://thinkchecksubmit.org)
and check Beall's List archive (https://beallslist.net).
